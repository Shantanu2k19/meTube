Don't Delete videos anonymously YouTube!

YouTube delete videos without telling the users who have saved those videos in their playlists. You can't even know the title of video that was deleted and hence can't find a replacement.
MeTube helps you to track the title of deleted videos

Try Demo

Features

If YouTube is your main source of Music, and you Save a lot of Lyrical videos in your Playlists, then MeTube is for you.
It becomes mildly annoying when YouTube deletes video and you have no idea which song you are missing.

Tracking

Find changes in your videos and playists at one place. You can see the videos and playlists added/deleted from your coount.

Customization

Everyone likes customization. Select the theme you wanna rock in your profile.

Playlists

All your playlists and details in one place. Get total Playlists count and number of songs in them.

Improvements

You can send feature suggestions and report bug in Profile tab.
